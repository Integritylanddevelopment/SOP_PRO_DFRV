🚨 SOP 1: Medical Emergency or Injury

▼

👥 People Involved

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/8 completed
✅ Check the scene for safety. Do not enter unsafe areas.
🚨 Call 911 immediately if: Person is unconscious, bleeding heavily, not breathing, suspected head/neck/spinal injury, chest pain, seizure, or possible stroke.
🩹 Provide basic aid if you are certified and it's safe to do so.
🛑 Keep the guest still and calm. Do not allow them to walk or move if injured.
🏃 Send another staff or volunteer to direct EMS to the location.
📞 Notify the GM and begin completing the Incident Report.
🏥 If injury is minor and does not require EMS: Offer first aid if certified, complete Incident Report, monitor guest.
🧽 Clean and disinfect any biohazard (blood, vomit, etc.) only after report is complete and safe to do so.

🚨 SOP 2: Criminal Incident (Assault, Theft, Harassment, Vandalism)

▼

👥 People Involved

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/10 completed
🚨 Call 911 immediately if violence is occurring or threat is imminent.
🛡️ Ensure your safety first. Do not confront suspects or put yourself at risk.
📱 Contact police immediately. Do not delay reporting criminal activity.
👀 Observe and document: suspect description, clothing, vehicle info, direction of travel.
🚫 Secure the scene. Do not allow contamination of evidence. Use barriers if available.
📝 Get witness statements and contact information. Write down exact quotes.
📷 Document evidence: Take photos of damage, scene, any physical evidence.
🎥 Check security cameras if available. Preserve footage immediately.
📞 Notify GM and complete incident report. Include all details and timeline.
👮 Cooperate fully with police. Provide all documentation and assist with investigation.

✍️ Employee Signature - Criminal Incident SOP

🚗 SOP 3: Vehicle Accident or Property Damage

▼

👥 People Involved

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/12 completed
🚨 Call 911 immediately if there are injuries or major damage requiring emergency response.
🛑 Secure the scene. Use cones or barriers to prevent further accidents or traffic hazards.
🩺 Check for injuries. Provide first aid if trained and safe to do so.
📷 Take photos from multiple angles: vehicles, damage, scene overview, license plates.
📋 Collect driver information: license, insurance, registration from all parties involved.
👀 Get witness information: names, phone numbers, and statements from anyone who saw accident.
📱 Call police if damage exceeds $1000, injuries occurred, or requested by any party.
🚫 Do not admit fault or make statements about who caused accident. Document facts only.
📞 Notify GM and our insurance company immediately with all collected information.
🗒️ Document road/weather conditions: note if wet, icy, construction, poor lighting, etc.
🚧 Check for property damage: signs, fencing, landscaping, utilities on resort property.
📝 Complete detailed incident report with all information, photos, and timeline.

✍️ Employee Signature - Vehicle Accident SOP

👤 SOP 4: Missing Person (Adult or Child)

▼

👥 People Involved

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/11 completed
🚨 IMMEDIATE: Call 911 if missing person is a child under 12 or vulnerable adult.
⏰ Get detailed information: when/where last seen, what wearing, medical conditions, medications.
📱 Get recent photo from family/friends if available. Note height, weight, distinguishing features.
🔍 Begin immediate search of common areas: bathrooms, pool, playground, office, store.
📢 Alert all staff via radio/phone to be on lookout. Provide description and last known location.
🚪 Check all entrances/exits. Ask gate attendants, review security cameras if available.
🚗 Check vehicles and RVs where person was staying. Look for unlocked vehicles.
👥 Organize systematic search with other guests/volunteers if safe and appropriate.
📞 Call 911 if not found within 30 minutes (sooner for children/vulnerable adults).
🌊 Check water areas thoroughly: pond, pool, hot tub - water emergencies are life threatening.
📝 Document all search efforts and timeline in detailed incident report for authorities.

✍️ Employee Signature - Missing Person SOP

🔊 SOP 5: Disturbances (Fights, Noise, Intoxication)

▼

👥 People Involved

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/9 completed
🚨 Call 911 immediately if violence is occurring or weapons are involved.
🛡️ Assess situation from safe distance. Do not confront intoxicated or violent individuals alone.
🗣️ Attempt verbal de-escalation if safe to do so. Stay calm, professional, non-threatening.
👥 Clear other guests from immediate area for their safety and to reduce audience effect.
📱 Call police if situation cannot be resolved peacefully or escalates further.
📹 Document everything: what was said, actions taken, witness information, damage.
🔊 For noise complaints: Politely inform guests of quiet hours and resort policies.
⚠️ Consider ejection from property if behavior continues or serious policy violations occurred.
📞 Notify GM and complete incident report with all details and follow-up actions needed.

✍️ Employee Signature - Disturbance SOP

🔥 SOP 6: Fire, Smoke, or Explosion

▼

🔥 Fire Emergency Details

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/8 completed
🚨 CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY - Every second counts in fire emergencies.
📢 EVACUATE all persons from immediate danger area. Sound alarm if available.
🧯 Use fire extinguisher ONLY if fire is small and you have clear escape route.
🏃 Assist with evacuation. Help elderly, disabled, or children evacuate first.
🚫 Close doors behind you to slow fire spread. Do not use elevators.
🚒 Meet fire department at entrance and direct them to fire location.
📊 Conduct headcount at designated assembly area. Report missing persons to fire department.
⛽ Turn off utilities (gas, electricity) if safe to do so and trained to do it.

✍️ Employee Signature - Fire Emergency SOP

⚠️ SOP 7: Hazardous Conditions or Utility Failure

▼

⚠️ Hazard Information

📸 Incident Documentation

Progress: 0/8 completed
⚡ Identify hazard type: electrical, chemical spill, gas leak, structural damage.
🚫 Secure area immediately. Prevent access by guests and staff using barriers/cones.
📞 Call appropriate emergency services: 911 for life threat, utility companies for service issues.
🏃 Evacuate if necessary. Move people upwind from chemical spills, away from electrical hazards.
📱 Notify utility companies: Electric (1-800-XXX), Gas (1-800-XXX), Water dept.
🛑 Turn off utilities if safe and trained to do so. Do not enter flooded areas with electricity.
🧯 Ventilate area if gas leak suspected. No smoking, sparks, or electrical switches.
📝 Document conditions, actions taken, and timeline in detailed incident report.

✍️ Employee Signature - Hazardous Conditions SOP

🚨 SOP 8: Sexual Assault or Abuse

▼

👥 Sensitive Information - Handle with Extreme Care

📸 Incident Documentation - CONFIDENTIAL

Progress: 0/9 completed
🚨 CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY - Sexual assault is a serious crime requiring immediate police response.
🤝 Support the victim. Believe them, listen without judgment, ensure their immediate safety.
🏥 Medical attention: Encourage victim to seek medical care even if no visible injuries.
🚫 Preserve evidence: Do not clean, move, or touch anything. Secure the scene.
🔒 Confidentiality: Only share information with police, management, and those with need to know.
📱 Provide victim with crisis resources: RAINN Hotline 1-800-656-4673, local victim services.
👮 Cooperate with police fully. Provide all requested information and documentation.
📞 Notify GM/Owner immediately. This requires highest level management involvement.
📝 Document everything carefully. Use victim's exact words. Store report securely.

✍️ Employee Signature - Sexual Assault SOP

🌪️ SOP 9: Severe Weather or Natural Disaster

▼

🌪️ Weather Emergency Details

📸 Weather Damage Documentation

Progress: 0/10 completed
📻 Monitor weather alerts via NOAA Weather Radio, apps, or emergency broadcasts.
📢 Alert all guests immediately via loudspeaker, door-to-door, or emergency notification system.
🏠 Direct guests to shelter: Bathhouse, office, or lowest floor of sturdy buildings.
🌪️ Tornado protocol: Get to interior room, lowest floor, away from windows. Crouch low, cover head.
💨 High wind protocol: Secure loose objects, stay away from trees, avoid RVs/tents.
🌊 Flooding protocol: Move to higher ground, avoid driving through water, turn off utilities.
📊 Account for all guests during and after event. Conduct headcount at shelter.
🚨 Call 911 for injuries, trapped persons, or major damage requiring emergency response.
📱 Communicate with authorities: Emergency management, utility companies, insurance.
📷 Document all damage with photos/video for insurance claims. Do not enter unsafe areas.

✍️ Employee Signature - Severe Weather SOP

💀 SOP 10: Death on Property

▼

💀 Death Incident Information - CONFIDENTIAL

📸 Scene Documentation - DO NOT DISTURB SCENE

Progress: 0/12 completed
🚨 CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY - All deaths must be reported to authorities regardless of apparent cause.
🩺 Check for signs of life if safe to do so. Do not assume death - begin CPR if trained.
🚫 DO NOT MOVE THE BODY or disturb the scene. This is now a potential crime scene.
🛑 Secure the scene immediately. Use barriers, tape, or staff to prevent contamination.
👥 Clear all guests/staff from immediate area. Provide privacy and preserve dignity.
📝 Document the scene with photos from multiple angles BEFORE authorities arrive.
👮 Cooperate fully with police and coroner. Provide all requested information.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Notify next of kin if known, or assist authorities in identification/notification.
📞 Notify GM/Owner immediately. This requires highest level management response.
📱 Contact insurance company and legal counsel as directed by management.
🤐 Maintain strict confidentiality. Do not discuss with guests, media, or unauthorized persons.
📋 Complete detailed incident report with exact timeline, all persons present, and circumstances.

✍️ Employee Signature - Death on Property SOP
